Transition Approach

For the editorial feel, I'm proposing staggered fade-in animations as sections come into view:

  • Section title fades in first (200ms)
  • Content blocks stagger in with slight delay between each (100ms offset)
  • No bounce or elastic effects — pure, intentional fade
  • Scroll-triggered via Intersection Observer (already in your code)
  • Happens once per section on first scroll into view
